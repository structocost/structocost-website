About StructoCost

Built on real project experience, not generic templates

StructoCost is an independent consultancy founded by licensed civil engineers with experience in structural design, cost planning, contract administration, and project delivery.

Our combined background spans residential development, land development works, infrastructure, and international engineering support. We bring together two disciplines that are often treated separately: structural design and cost control.

That integration allows us to help clients reduce redesigns, minimize waste, align scope with budget, and make more informed decisions before construction begins.
